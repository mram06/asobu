Buddy Bottle

Water bowls, feed & snack bowls for dogs, and even a warm and cold tumbler for me!

Buddy Tumbler is made by Canadian drinkware brand Asobu.

Asobu, a premium drinkware brand, was born in 2010 by ADNART, a Canadian company founded in 1998. ADNART is a drinkware manufacturing company that has collaborated with many global companies for over 20 years to introduce mugs, tumblers, and coffee makers to the market. All products of ASOBU, made by ADNART, which has received steady love calls from premium companies around the world, are created with its own design and technology development, and most of the products have Worldwide Patents.

A tumbler for both pets and people

The container for beverage and the water bowl for the dog are separated so that the pet and the person can drink water together.

Bouble stainless tumbler

It has a double stainless steel structure, so water droplets do not form on the surface and the temperature of the beverage is less affected by the outer wall of the cup.

Sealing power for safe use

Even if you put in your bag, you don’t have to worry about leaking water, so it’s convenient to carry.

Detachable water bowl

A detachable silicone water bowl is designed at the bottom of the tumbler so that you can pour water for your pet.

To go out with your dog, you only need one Asobu Buddy!

There are two detachable bowls at the bottom of the tumbler buddy. There is a food bowl for carrying food and a water bowl for pouring water. You can carry 2 bowls along with the tumbler, so it is really convenient for outdoor activities such as walking and camping. The bottle for the beverage and the bowl used by the dog are separated, so the dog and the owner can share one tumbler. No need to bring anything.
